How to use Budget

Add a budget to get real-time feedback on your spendings vs. your budget. ExpenSe displays information such as percentage of budget used, cost/day this month, as well as budget remaining. It'll also tell you when your budget will reset. Updating your budget will not change the reset date, so don't worry!
