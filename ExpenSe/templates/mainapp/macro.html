How to use Macros

To add a macro, simply key in a letter followed by the name you want that letter to represent. For example, if you wanted to make a macro for groceries, enter ggroceries in the box on the right. Now whenever you want to ExpenSe a groceries for $15, you only have to enter g15 on the dashboard.
