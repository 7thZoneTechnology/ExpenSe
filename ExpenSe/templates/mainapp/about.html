Expenses

  • Easily record your expenses from the dashboard. Just enter your macro+expense amount. So if your macro was c: car, enter in c99.99 for a $99.99 car expense.

  • View all your expenses right on your dashboard. Editing and deleting expenses is also supported through the Edit button for each expense.

Macros

  • A macro is a hotkey for a type of expense. For example, f=food will set the macro f to the expense type food. Set, add and delete macros to make recording expenses easier.

  • The default macros may be overwritten, but not deleted.

Budget

  • Set a monthly budget for yourself. Expen$e will keep track of how much you've used this month.

  • A cost per day figure is also calculated for you to easily view your daily cost of living. It gets more accurate the longer you use Expen$e.
